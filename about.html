About

Sophia Pinto

I'm a former educator turned product thinker with a focus on edtech. After completing an AmeriCorps Teach For America placement teaching 4th grade math at a Title I school in Atlanta, I became fascinated by the gap between what students needed and what existing tools were built to do. SparkStep is my attempt to close that gap — a speculative product case study documenting the full design process from classroom observation to wireframe. I'm now looking for a role on a product team where I can bring that same combination of user empathy, systems thinking, and mission-driven design.

I'm always interested in a chat — connect with me here.
